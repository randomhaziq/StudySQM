### 🎯 Learning Objectives
Students should be able to:
1. Explain the importance of software metrics and measurement in quality management.
1. Apply software process measurement to monitor and improve development activities.
1. Apply software product measurement to assess quality attributes such as complexity and maintainability.
1. Differentiate between process metrics and product metrics.
1. Identify common metrics used to evaluate software development performance.
1. Apply the Goal Question Metric (GQM) approach to design measurement frameworks.
