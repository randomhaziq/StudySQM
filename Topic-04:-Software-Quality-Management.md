# Software Quality Management
### SQM Processes, Software Quality Tasks, and SQM in Software Development Methodology

---

## Goal

To provide students with an understanding of Software Quality Management (SQM), its processes, quality-related tasks, and how quality management activities are integrated throughout the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) to ensure the delivery of high-quality software products.

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

Students should be able to:

1. Explain the concept, objectives, and importance of Software Quality Management (SQM) in software development.
2. Describe the key SQM processes, including Quality Planning, Quality Assurance (QA), and Quality Control (QC), and their roles in ensuring software quality.
3. Apply software quality management practices and quality-related tasks throughout the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) to improve software quality and reduce defects.

---

## 📌 Topics to Cover

### 1. Introduction to Software Quality Management (SQM)

- Definition of Software Quality Management (SQM)
- Objectives of SQM
- Benefits of implementing SQM

### 2. SQM Processes

#### Quality Planning

- Establishing quality objectives
- Quality standards and procedures
- Quality metrics
- Quality management plan

#### Quality Assurance (QA)

- Process-oriented approach
- Defect prevention
- Quality audits
- Process improvement
- Compliance with standards

#### Quality Control (QC)

- Product-oriented approach
- Defect detection
- Verification and validation
- Software testing activities

### 3. Software Quality Tasks

- Requirements review
- Design review
- Code review
- Walkthroughs
- Technical reviews
- Inspections
- Testing activities
- Defect management
- Configuration management
- Quality measurement and metrics
- Quality audits

### 4. Applying SQM Throughout the SDLC

#### Requirements Phase

- Requirements validation
- Requirements review

#### Design Phase

- Design evaluation
- Design review

#### Development Phase

- Coding standards
- Code review
- Static analysis

#### Testing Phase

- Unit testing
- Integration testing
- System testing
- User Acceptance Testing (UAT)

#### Deployment and Maintenance Phase

- Release verification
- Defect tracking
- Continuous improvement

### 5. QA vs QC

| Quality Assurance (QA) | Quality Control (QC) |
|------------------------|---------------------|
| Focuses on processes | Focuses on products |
| Prevents defects | Detects defects |
| Proactive approach | Reactive approach |
| Audits and standards | Testing and inspections |
