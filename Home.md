# STTPK3123 Software Quality Management

### Synopsis

This course will provide the students with theoretical knowledge about concepts of software quality, quality models,
software quality standards and methodologies used in the software industry. The principles of software development and management are presented with special emphasis on the processes and activities of quality assurance. In addition, the role of standards, principles, and procedures is discussed, with examples drawn from ISO, CMMI, and other process models and standards. This course provides practical knowledge of a variety of quality assurance techniques and an understanding of some of the trade-offs between techniques.

### Course Learning Outcome (CLO):
Upon completion of the course, students are able to:
1. Describe the concept and techniques of Software Quality Management (C2)
2. Explain the implementation of Software Quality Management (C2)
3. Explain the process of evaluating a software to ensure it is compliance with the quality standard (A3)
4. Apply the appropriate concepts and techniques to evaluate a software (C3)
