# Software Quality Plan (SQP)

---

## Goal

To provide students with an understanding of the Software Quality Plan (SQP), its key components, and its role in ensuring software quality throughout a project. Students will also learn the differences between a Quality Plan and a Project Plan and how both contribute to successful software development.

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

Students should be able to:

1. Describe key components of the Software Quality Plan (SQP).
2. Differentiate between a Quality Plan and a Project Plan.

---

## 📌 Topics to Cover

### 1. Introduction to Software Quality Plan (SQP)

- Definition of Software Quality Plan
- Purpose of a Software Quality Plan
- Importance of quality planning in software projects

### 2. Key Components of a Software Quality Plan

#### Quality Goals

- Definition and purpose
- Examples of quality goals

#### Quality Objectives

- Definition and purpose
- SMART quality objectives

#### Quality Standards

- Internal and external quality standards
- Industry standards (ISO 9001, ISO/IEC 25010)

#### Quality Assurance Activities

- Process reviews
- Audits
- Compliance checks

#### Quality Control Activities

- Testing activities
- Inspections
- Defect management

#### Roles and Responsibilities

- Project Manager
- Quality Assurance Team
- Developers
- Testers

#### Quality Metrics

- Defect Density
- Test Coverage
- Customer Satisfaction

### 3. Quality Plan vs Project Plan

#### Project Plan

- Project scope
- Schedule and milestones
- Budget and resources
- Risk management

#### Quality Plan

- Quality goals
- Quality objectives
- Quality activities
- Quality standards
- Quality metrics

#### Comparison Between Quality Plan and Project Plan

| Aspect | Project Plan | Quality Plan |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Focus | Project execution | Software quality |
| Purpose | Deliver project successfully | Ensure quality requirements are met |
| Contents | Scope, schedule, budget, resources | Quality goals, standards, metrics, QA/QC activities |
| Responsibility | Project Manager | QA Team / Project Team |
| Success Measure | Project completion | Product quality achievement |

### 4. Importance of Quality Planning

- Reducing defects
- Improving customer satisfaction
- Supporting continuous improvement
- Ensuring compliance with standards

## 💪 Hands-on Activity

### Activity: Developing a Simple Software Quality Plan

#### Identify SQP Components

Given a software project case study (e.g., Library Management System), students identify:

- Quality goals
- Quality objectives
- Quality standards
- QA activities
- QC activities
