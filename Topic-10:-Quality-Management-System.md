### 🎯 Learning Objectives
Students should be able to:
1. Explain the concept and importance of a Quality Management System (QMS) in software development.
1. Identify and describe the key elements of QMS.
1. Explain how the Rayleigh model framework supports quality planning and defect prediction.
1. Describe reliability growth models and their role in continuous quality improvement.
1. Apply complexity metrics to evaluate software quality and maintainability.
1. Analyse customer satisfaction data to improve software quality.
1. Relate QMS practices to real-world standards such as ISO 9001 and ISO/IEC 25010.
