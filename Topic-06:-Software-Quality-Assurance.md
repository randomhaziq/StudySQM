### Goal
To equip students with a solid understanding of Software Quality Assurance concepts, processes, and practices, enabling them to plan, monitor, and evaluate software quality activities throughout the software development lifecycle.


### 🎯 Learning Objectives
Students should be able to:
1. Explain the role and importance of Software Quality Assurance (SQA) in software development
2. Prepare a basic Software Quality Assurance (SQA) Plan
3. Distinguish between different types of audits in software projects
4. Describe audit roles and responsibilities.
5. Explain the audit process and standard procedures used in SQA


### 📌 Topics to Cover                               
1. Introduction to Software Quality Assurance
   - Definition of SQA
   - Difference between SQA, QC, and Testing
   - Importance of SQA in SDLC

2. Quality Tasks in Software Projects
   - Process monitoring
   - Standards compliance
   - Documentation review
   - Configuration management
   - Risk identification related to quality

3. Software Quality Goals
   - Functional correctness
   - Reliability
   - Maintainability
   - Usability
   - Performance
   - Compliance with standards (e.g., ISO/IEC)

4. Software Quality Assurance (SQA) Plan
   - Purpose of an SQA Plan
   - Typical contents:
   - Quality objectives
   - Standards and procedures
   - Roles and responsibilities
   - Review and audit schedules
   - Metrics and reporting

5. Audit Types in Software Quality
   - Process Audit
   - Product Audit
   - System Audit
   - Internal vs External Audit

6. Audit Roles and Responsibilities
   - Auditor
   - Lead Auditor
   - Auditee (project team)
   - Management responsibilities

7. Audit Process and Procedures
   - Audit planning
   - Audit execution
   - Evidence collection
   - Audit reporting
   - Corrective and preventive actions
   - Follow-up activities
